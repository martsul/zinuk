Exam Part Intro

At the end of the test, the scores in the three areas (verbal reasoning, quantitative reasoning and English) and general scores will be displayed: a general multidisciplinary score, a general score with a verbal emphasis and a score with a quantitative emphasis. In the real test, the scores will not appear at the end of the test, but only with the official publication of the scores

The test scores are only an estimate of the psychometric exam scores, and do not serve as a substitute for the scores of a standardized psychometric exam. As a reminder, unlike in a real psychometric exam, in this test the score in the verbal reasoning area does not include a score on a writing assignment.
